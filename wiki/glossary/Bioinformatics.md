The scientific discipline that seeks to solve biological problems at the molecular level through the **computational processing of information** derived — directly or indirectly — from living organisms.

Types of information processed include: genomic sequences (DNA, genomes, exomes), protein sequences (cDNA), 3D protein structures (NMR, crystallography), medical images, blood/fluid measurements, molecular interaction networks, evolutionary data, and physiological signals.

Bioinformatics underpins every layer of the [[Omics landscape]]: [[Genomics]], transcriptomics, proteomics, and metabolomics.

# TLDR
Solving biological problems with computational tools applied to molecular-scale data.
